Payment Methods
---------------

Membership fees can be paid via:

   - Direct Debit: With completed SEPA mandate in the members online profile.
   - iDEAL, [Belgian iDEAL] or Credit Card: Via the unique link in the members online profile.

   *Note: Direct Debit provides a discounted membership fee as listed above. International Members with an address that is outside of the SEPA region are eligible to pay the discounted rate via Credit Card or Bank Transfer.*


For more information on membership fees or to apply for membership, please contact secretary@kna-rnas.nl.
