80-jaar-vrijheid
=================

This document commemorates the 80th anniversary of freedom, highlighting the significance of this milestone in relation to the KNA-RNAS Library. It reflects on the historical context, the events leading up to this anniversary, and the impact it has had on the library and its community.

Key Highlights
--------------

- Historical events leading to the 80th anniversary of freedom.
- Contributions of the KNA-RNAS Library to the preservation of astronomical knowledge during this period.
- Celebratory events and publications related to the anniversary.

For more information, please refer to the official communications and publications sections of the KNA-RNAS Library documentation.
