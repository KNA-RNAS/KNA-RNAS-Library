Royal Designation of the KNA-RNAS
===============================================

.. document-of-record::

.. document-status::
   :approved: true
   :approved_in: Centenary Board Meeting 2018
   :notary_stamp: 2018-10-15

The Royal Netherlands Astronomical Society (Koninklijke Nederlandse Astronomen Club, KNA-RNAS) was granted the royal designation by His Majesty King Willem-Alexander of the Netherlands on October 2018, on the 100th Anniversary of the society. This designation is a recognition of the society's long-standing contributions to the field of astronomy and its commitment to promoting astronomical knowledge and education in the Netherlands.
