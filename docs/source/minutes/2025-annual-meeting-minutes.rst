Minutes of the Royal Netherlands Astronomical Society (Koninklijke Nederlandse Astronomenclub) Annual General Meeting 2025
==========================================================================================================================

Berg en Dal, Netherlands

Total Members Present at the opening of the meeting: 13 plus the following

Board Members Present: Marijke Haverkorn (chairperson, incoming), Jake Noel-Storr (secretary), Ralph Wijers (treasurer, outgoing), Steven Rieder (treasurer, incoming), Sven De Rijcke, Katie Mulrey

External Audit Committee Members Present: Vince Icke, Frank Pijpers

Apologies Received: Peter Barthel (chairperson, outgoing), Rudolf Le Poole, Dieter Heymann

Preamble:
---------

The Chair shared the mission of the society as reads:

The society wants to be THE platform for professional and former astronomers, it fosters contacts between them, and stimulates and supports actions towards the societal goals concerning the promotion of astronomy in The Netherlands and Flanders.

The one-and-only professional organization of astronomers and space researchers in NL/B

Please visit the website www.kna-rnas.nl

Opening and adoption of the agenda
----------------------------------

Meeting opened by the chair at 15:20h on 27th May, 2025 in the Fletcher Landgoed Hotel Holthurnsche Hof, Berg en Dal, Netherlands, during the 80th Netherlands Astronomy Conference, organized by our Radboud University Colleagues.

1. **Agenda as announced adopted without objection**

Board announcements
-------------------

2. Apologies given as above

3. New Board Member Steven Rieder has been acting-co-treasurer, and will be treasurer from this meeting onwards with Ralph Wijers as co-treasurer.

4. Minutes of the business meeting of May 14, 2024

   1. Approved without objection

5. Minutes of the special meeting of December 2, 2024

   1. Approved without objection

Annual report 2024 of the secretary
-----------------------------------

6.  The 2024 Board of the RNAS/KNA consisted of Peter Barthel (chair), Jake Noel-Storr (secretary), Jan Lub (Treasurer until May), Ralph Wijers (Treasurer since May), Sven De Rijcke, Katie Mulrey, Jacqueline Hodge, and Marijke Haverkorn

7.  The number of members remained over 500.

8.  There were two activities, the 79th NAC, and the musical celebration in Haarlem of asteroid (12639) Tonkoopman.

9.  The planned RNAS/NOVA Teacher’s Day had to be postponed.

10. A standardized website for the annual NACs was designed and installed.

11. The 79th Netherlands Astronomers Conference in Egmond aan Zee was a great success. Well attended: ~250 participants. The first Kees de Jager (education) prize was awarded to Alex de Koter.

12. A small rise in membership fees was discussed at the NAC Business Meeting, and later accepted during an on-line extra-ordinary member meeting.

13. Migration of website and (member + financial) administration was a major effort, which is nearly completed. An Indico server is now hosted for RNAS for this and future meetings.

14. A third Newsletter was e-mailed to all members. Continued focus on rejuvenation and optimal representation of its community, and on improving the Society’s value for all members.

15. The Board is in regular contact with the EAS, and encourages EAS-activities by the RNAS members.

16. The Board expresses sincere thanks to all who have contributed to and supported the Society.

17. Updates mid-2025

    1. Membership as of today is at 558 Members (noting that Lost Members and Members without status still contribute somewhat to this figure)

    2. Registration for the NAC this year was 241 participants

    3. Membership database migration in good shape, billing and other handling underway

    4. Website member areas will be developed (working groups; communities; blogs) Inputs welcomed

    5. Next Generation Astronomers – Social Media Takeover for 2 weeks; plus a featured page on the website introducing you to the community

    6. Make the website a community space for Dutch Astronomy

    7. Electronic Documents when approved will be archived in Zenodo (CERN)

18. **Motion to approve the report and updates moved by the Secretary**

    1. Passed without objection
